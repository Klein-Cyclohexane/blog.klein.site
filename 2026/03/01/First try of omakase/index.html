First try of omakase

I've always been curious about the texture of sashimi. I'd only tried salmon before, so today I went with my sister and brother-in-law to try omakase.

The rain was pouring down heavily, and we barely made it to the restaurant.

The restaurant exudes a distinctly Japanese ambiance. The head chef silently preps ingredients until he introduces the dishes to us—only then do I realize he isn't Japanese.( )

There are many dishes, though the portions aren't large, but it's very satisfying.

I finally discovered that raw sea urchin is actually sweet, just like ice cream.

My absolute favorite is the peony shrimp and sea urchin tempura with shiso leaves, paired with black truffle and wagyu beef sukiyaki.

The other dishes were beautifully plated and tasted delicious.

A Satisfying Day!😋
